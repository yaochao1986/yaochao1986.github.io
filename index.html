Biography

I am currently working at School of Computer & Communication Engineering, University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB) as an assistant Professor. Previously, I have received B.Eng., B.A. and Ph.D from Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in 2020, supervised by Prof. Yao ZHAO. My research interests lie at Video Coding, Image Processing and Computer Vision.
